Easy Pumpkin Cake Recipe

This is the perfect cake for you festive fall!

This classic recipe takes everything we love about the fall and brings it together into one delicious dessert! Feeling excited about the Autumn season? Then look no further! This will be the tastiest treat you've had all year!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups vegetable oil
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 3 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups solid pack pumpking puree
  • 0.5 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 C). Grease and clour a 10 inch tube pan. Sift together flour, soda, baking powder, cinnamon and salt. Set aside.

  2. In a large bowl, combine oil and sugar. Add eggs one at a time and mix well. Add the flour mixture and beat until smooth. Add nuts and pumpking and blend until smooth. Pour batter into greased 10 inch tube pan.

  3. Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 1 hour or until a toothpick inserted into the center of cake comes out clean. Cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out into a wire rack and finish cooling. Dust with confectioners sugar before serving.
